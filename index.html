Welcome to Creative Cope


Welcome to Creative Cope Art Therapy.

Our talented team combines art and psychotherapy into a variety of therapudic classes in order to help those with mental illness
cope in a creative way. We are currently offering two group therapy classes as well as one on one sessions. You'll find a little bit
about them below. If you'd like more information or have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out. You can find our contact
information on the "GET IN TOUCH" page.


Classes offered

One on One Session Information

Individual Sessions

Group therapy isn't for everyone, and that's okay!
If you prefer one on one therapy, our individual
sessions may be right for you. Paints, pencil crayons,
and clay are mediums often used during these sessions.

Bullet Journaling Class Information

Bullet Journaling

Journaling is an incredible tool for sorting and
acknowledging feelings and emotions. This class is great
for anyone seeking a more structures and creative outlet.
Each class ends with an optional "sharing circle".

Knitting Circle Information

Knitting Circle

Knitting is perfect for keeping your mind and hands busy.
For these classes we play music, knit some cool stuff, and
discuss some CBT tools that help with an array of mental
health disorders.
